Why Us!

What we do

We are a provider of choice for clients looking for quality horticultural produce. Our broad range of products include; Flowers, beans, snow peas, sugar snaps, garden peas, avocadoes, Mangoes, Passion fruits, baby corn and sugar cane among others. Fresh is the name and whatever we can grow and or consolidate, you shall get it fresh! We are built on a heritage of quality products, timely delivery, excellence and industry knowledge.
