CCTV Products

Introducing Our Range of Cutting-Edge CCTV Products

We're dedicated to providing state-of-the-art CCTV solutions tailored to meet your security needs. Explore our diverse range of high-performance CCTV products designed to deliver unparalleled surveillance capabilities and peace of mind.

1. Dome CCTV Camera
The Dome CCTV Camera is a versatile and discreet surveillance solution suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. Its compact design and dome-shaped casing make it ideal for installations where aesthetics are important, such as retail stores, hotels, and office buildings. With features like infrared night vision, wide-angle lens options, and vandal-resistant construction, the Dome CCTV Camera provides reliable 24/7 monitoring, ensuring that your premises remain secure at all times.

Key Features:

Vandal-resistant design

Infrared night vision for low-light conditions

Wide-angle lens options for comprehensive coverage

Weatherproof casing for outdoor installations

High-definition resolution for crisp and clear footage

2. Bullet CCTV Camera
The Bullet CCTV Camera is a popular choice for outdoor surveillance applications due to its rugged design and long-range capabilities. With its sleek, cylindrical shape and adjustable mounting bracket, the Bullet Camera is easy to install and can be positioned for optimal coverage of parking lots, warehouses, and construction sites. Equipped with advanced features such as motion detection, remote pan/tilt/zoom control, and built-in microphones, the Bullet CCTV Camera offers reliable performance and peace of mind for property owners and business managers.

Key Features:

Long-range infrared night vision

Weatherproof and vandal-resistant construction

Remote pan/tilt/zoom control for flexible monitoring

Motion detection and customizable alerts

Built-in microphone for audio surveillance

3.PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) CCTV Camera
The PTZ CCTV Camera is a versatile surveillance solution that offers unmatched flexibility and control. With its motorized pan, tilt, and zoom functions, this camera can cover large areas with precision and detail, making it ideal for monitoring expansive outdoor spaces such as parking lots, stadiums, and industrial complexes. Whether you need to track moving objects or zoom in on specific areas of interest, the PTZ CCTV Camera provides high-quality footage and real-time monitoring capabilities, empowering you to keep a close watch on your property at all times.

Key Features:

Motorized pan, tilt, and zoom functions for precise control

High-definition resolution for detailed footage

Auto-tracking feature to follow moving objects

Preset tours and patterns for automated surveillance

Weatherproof and durable construction for outdoor use

4. Wireless CCTV Camera System
The Wireless CCTV Camera System offers the convenience of hassle-free installation without the need for complicated wiring. Perfect for residential properties, small businesses, and rental properties, this plug-and-play system includes wireless cameras that communicate with a central hub or DVR via Wi-Fi, allowing for easy setup and configuration. With features like remote viewing, motion detection alerts, and expandable storage options, the Wireless CCTV Camera System provides reliable security coverage without the need for extensive cabling or professional installation.

Key Features:

Wireless connectivity for easy installation

Remote viewing via smartphone, tablet, or computer

Motion detection alerts sent to your mobile device

Expandable storage options for extended recording

Weatherproof cameras for indoor and outdoor use

5. 360-Degree Panoramic CCTV Camera
The 360-Degree Panoramic CCTV Camera offers comprehensive coverage of large indoor spaces such as warehouses, retail stores, and office lobbies. With its fisheye lens and wide-angle field of view, this camera can monitor an entire area without blind spots, eliminating the need for multiple cameras and reducing installation costs. Featuring advanced dewarping technology and panoramic stitching, the 360-Degree Panoramic CCTV Camera delivers immersive and distortion-free footage, allowing you to capture every detail with clarity and precision.

Key Features:

360-degree panoramic view without blind spots

Fisheye lens and wide-angle field of view

Advanced dewarping technology for distortion-free footage

Remote pan/tilt/zoom control for flexible monitoring

Multiple viewing modes for customized surveillance configurations
